Frequently Asked Questions

Who is Clara for?

Clara is designed for senior care placement agencies, care coordinators, and families searching for assisted living, adult family homes, memory care, and other senior care facilities in Washington State.

What's included in the free plan?

Search 5,200+ licensed facilities on an interactive map, view inspection severity scores, generate PDF comparison reports, and track your favorites.

What does Premium add?

AI chat, full inspection reports with AI analysis, facility contact info, automated voice calls with live transcription, SMS outreach, and a full kanban pipeline to manage your placements.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Monthly plans can be cancelled anytime — you keep access until the end of your billing period. Annual plan cancellations are prorated at the monthly rate.

Is there a free trial?

The free plan is permanently free with no time limit. You can upgrade to Premium when you need advanced features like AI chat, automated calls, and SMS outreach.
