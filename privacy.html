Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

Cut Boring Work ("we", "us") operates cutboringwork.com and the Clara senior care assistant at clara.cutboringwork.com. This policy describes what data we collect and how we use it.

What We Collect

  • Google account info: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture. We use this solely to authenticate you and personalize your experience.
  • Conversations: Messages you send to Clara are stored to maintain your session history and improve search results. Conversations are tied to your account and not shared with third parties.
  • Search activity: Facility searches and saved preferences are stored to provide search history and personalized results.

What We Don't Collect

  • We do not collect health records, medical information, or protected health information (PHI).
  • We do not use tracking pixels, third-party analytics, or advertising cookies.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with anyone.

Facility Data

Facility information displayed in Clara comes from public sources, primarily the Washington State Department of Social and Health Services (DSHS) and Google Places. We do not guarantee the accuracy or completeness of this data. Always verify information directly with facilities.

Cookies

We use a session cookie to keep you signed in. No advertising or tracking cookies are used.

Data Storage

Your data is stored on servers in the United States (Hillsboro, Oregon). We use encryption in transit (TLS) and take reasonable measures to protect your data.

Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing howto@cutboringwork.com. We will process your request within 30 days.

Voice Calls

When you use Clara's call feature, Clara places a call to the facility on your behalf using an AI voice assistant. These calls are recorded and transcribed automatically. Call recordings and transcriptions are stored on our servers and associated with your account. Facility operators are informed at the beginning of each call that the call is being recorded. Recordings are retained for 90 days unless you request earlier deletion.

SMS Communications

Clara can send SMS messages to facilities on your behalf through our communications provider, Twilio. Message content is stored and associated with your account. Standard messaging rates may apply. Facility phone numbers are sourced from public records.

Facility Portal

Facility operators who claim their listing provide their email address, phone number, contact name, role, and bed availability information. This data is displayed to families searching for care on Clara. Facility operators can update or remove their information at any time through the portal.

AI Processing

Your conversations, search queries, and interactions with Clara are processed by third-party AI models to generate responses, facility summaries, inspection analyses, and call transcriptions. We do not sell your conversation data. Conversations are stored to maintain your session history and improve the service.

Payment Processing

Subscription payments are processed by Stripe, Inc. We do not store your credit card information. Stripe's privacy policy governs their handling of your payment data.

Activity Logging

We log user actions (such as searches, logins, and feature usage), IP addresses, and browser information for security, debugging, and service improvement purposes.

Third-Party Data Sources

Clara displays data from Washington State DSHS (facility licenses and inspections) and Google Places (ratings and reviews). We do not control the accuracy of third-party data.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data by emailing howto@cutboringwork.com. Requests are processed within 30 days. California residents may exercise their rights under the CCPA by contacting us at the same address.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.

Contact

Questions? Email us at howto@cutboringwork.com.
